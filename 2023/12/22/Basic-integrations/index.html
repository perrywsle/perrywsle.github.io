Inverse of derivatives

Integrations are just inverse of derivatives:

∫abf(x)dx=−∫baf(x)dx\int_{a}^{b}f(x)\mathrm{d}x=-\int_{b}^{a}f(x)\mathrm{d}x ∫ab​f(x)dx=−∫ba​f(x)dx

Common integrals

Integrals ❗
$$\int x^n\mathrm{d}x=\frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1}+c$$ $$\int \sec^2x\mathrm{d}x=\tan x +c$$
$$\int x^{-1}\mathrm{d}x=\ln \mid x\mid + c$$ $$\int \sec x \tan x \mathrm{d}x=\sec x +c$$
$$\int \frac{1}{ax+b}\mathrm{d}x=\frac{1}{a}\ln \mid ax+b\mid +c$$ $$\int \csc x \cot x \mathrm{d}x=-\csc x +c$$
