Substitution

∫abf(g(x))g′(x)dx=∫g(a)g(b)f(u)du\int_{a}^{b}f(g(x))g'(x)\mathrm{d}x=\int_{g(a)}^{g(b)}f(u)\mathrm{d}u ∫ab​f(g(x))g′(x)dx=∫g(a)g(b)​f(u)du

  • u = g(x)
  • du = g’(x)dx

Integration by parts

∫abudv=[uv]ab−∫abvdu\int_{a}^{b} u \mathrm{d}v =[uv]_{a}^{b} - \int_{a}^{b} v \mathrm{d}u ∫ab​udv=[uv]ab​−∫ab​vdu

  • choose u and dv
  • compute du with differentiation
  • compute v with integration
  • generally put more complicated shit as dv and easier shit as u
    Derivation of integration by parts:
    Product rule states that: $$\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x}uv = v\frac{\mathrm{d}u}{\mathrm{d}x} \times u\frac{\mathrm{d}v}{\mathrm{d}x}$$
    Thus: $$\mathrm{d}(uv)=v\mathrm{d}u \times u\mathrm{d}v$$
    Integrate both sides: $$\int \mathrm{d}(uv) = \int v\mathrm{d}u \times \int u\mathrm{d}v=uv$$

∫udv=uv−∫vdu\int u\mathrm{d}v=uv-\int v\mathrm{d}u ∫udv=uv−∫vdu

Example:

∫35ln⁡(x)dx=[xln⁡(x)]35−∫35xxdx=[xln⁡(x)−x]35=5ln⁡(5)−3ln⁡(3)−2\begin{aligned}&\int_{3}^{5}\ln (x)\mathrm{d}x\\&=[x\ln(x)]_{3}^{5}-\int_{3}^{5}\frac{x}{x}\mathrm{d}x\\&=[x\ln (x)-x]_{3}^{5}\\&=5\ln(5)-3\ln(3)-2\end{aligned} ​∫35​ln(x)dx=[xln(x)]35​−∫35​xx​dx=[xln(x)−x]35​=5ln(5)−3ln(3)−2​

  • u = ln(x)
  • dv = dx
  • du = 1/x dx
  • v = x
