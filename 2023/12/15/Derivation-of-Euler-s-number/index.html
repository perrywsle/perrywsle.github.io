ddx2x=2x+△x−2x△x=2x(2△x−1△x)2△x−1△x≊0.6931...ddx3x=3x+△x−3x△x=3x(3△x−1△x)3△x−1△x≊1.0986...::ddx8x=8x+△x−8x△x=8x(8△x−1△x)8△x−1△x≊2.0794...\begin{aligned} &\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x}2^x\\ &=\frac{2^{x+ \triangle x}-2^x}{\triangle x}\\ &=2^x(\frac{2^{\triangle x}-1}{\triangle x})\\ &\frac{2^{\triangle x}-1}{\triangle x}\approxeq 0.6931...\\ &\\ &\\ &\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x}3^x\\ &=\frac{3^{x+ \triangle x}-3^x}{\triangle x}\\ &=3^x(\frac{3^{\triangle x}-1}{\triangle x})\\ &\frac{3^{\triangle x}-1}{\triangle x}\approxeq 1.0986...\\ &:\\ &:\\ &\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x}8^x\\ &=\frac{8^{x+ \triangle x}-8^x}{\triangle x}\\ &=8^x(\frac{8^{\triangle x}-1}{\triangle x})\\ &\frac{8^{\triangle x}-1}{\triangle x}\approxeq 2.0794...\\ \end{aligned} ​dxd​2x=△x2x+△x−2x​=2x(△x2△x−1​)△x2△x−1​≊0.6931...dxd​3x=△x3x+△x−3x​=3x(△x3△x−1​)△x3△x−1​≊1.0986...::dxd​8x=△x8x+△x−8x​=8x(△x8△x−1​)△x8△x−1​≊2.0794...​

In the sequence above,it is found out that the derivative of a base to the power of a variable is: $$base^{variable} \times constant$$

When the base is e, the constant = 1
