Acid base equilibrium

7A: pH value of strong and weak acid

  • strong acid ionises completely in water
  • weak acid ionises partially in water

7B: buffer solutions

  • buffer solution is made by mixing weak acid with its conjugate base, forming a system of dynamic equilibrium
  • when acid is added, the conjugate base amount is reduce because it accepted the protons,
    • thus the system shifts to right, produce more base, indirectly minimizing pH change
  • when base is added, the weak acid reacts with the hydroxide ions, decreasing in amount
    • thus the system shifts to left, producing more weak acid, indirectly minimizing the pH change
Precautions
  • Careful with dilutions
  • Rinse apparatus before dilute
  • Rinse pH meter‚Äôs probe everytime after/before use
